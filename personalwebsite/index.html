CHRISTINE HONG

ABOUT

Christine is currently a sophomore at Harvard College intending to concentrate in Economics with a secondary in Psychology. She works at Harvard's Innovation Lab as a Marketing Intern with Autumnate, serves as an Analyst for Harvard Consulting on Business and Environment, is a graduate of the Leadership Institute's Leads Program, serves as an Associate for Harvard Ventures, and plays violin in the Harvard Radcliffe Orchestra and in the Brattle Street Chamber Players.

CAUSE

I'm inspired by the potential of music to change lives. Playing the violin for 12 years has allowed me to explore the many facets of my identity throughout my development while being the greatest teacher of empathy. Music has served as a gateway for me to deepen my understanding of the human condition and foundationally governs my interactions with others. I deeply believe that music has a ability to break down the barriers that we humans build around ourselves and between each other and that with this knowledge, we may have hope for a brighter future.
